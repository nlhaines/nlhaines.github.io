Personal Finance Package for R

A package for simulating personal finance outcomes. Simulations are month-by-month and account for changes in macro-economic factors such as inflation, interest rates, and more. You define your investments, income streams, monthly spending, how much you'd like to invest in the stock market monthly, and how you'd like to allocate your cash between currencies. Users can define targets for key metrics to see how long they will take to reach under different scenarios, or see where those metrics will be at the end of the simulation period. Development is ongoing and suggestions are welcome.

The world is complex, and everything is interconnected.
